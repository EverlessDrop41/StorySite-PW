Welcome to a writers webste

Peter Wakefield is a retired church minister, who currently enjoys life in the West Midlands, England with his wife Christine. He is a keen short story writer who was delighted in 2012 to be chosen as the winner of a prestigious competition organised by Writing Magazine.

This website will show off some of his short stories, and give information on his published books. You are free (and encouraged) to leave comments on the stories.
